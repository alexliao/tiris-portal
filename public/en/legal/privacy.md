# Privacy Policy

*Last updated November 18, 2025*

## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how Tiris Portal collects, uses, and safeguards information when you explore our machine-learning powered quantitative trading experience, create an account, or connect exchanges through our APIs. The policy covers data flowing through the landing page, performance demonstrations, authentication flows, exchange-binding wizards, and future onboarding features included in our product roadmap.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the minimum data required to deliver the education-first product roadmap:

- Account information such as name, email address, locale, OAuth provider metadata, and verification status
- Usage telemetry (page visits, navigation paths, language preference, feature adoption) that helps us validate UX goals outlined in the front-end specification
- Trading configuration inputs including selected exchanges, trading names, slider values for paper trading allocations, and contextual form data
- Device information (browser type, operating system, viewport size) needed to ensure responsive performance testing criteria
- Support correspondence, product feedback, or questionnaire responses that you voluntarily share

## 3. How We Use Information
Data is processed to deliver and improve the services defined in the brief, system definition, and architecture documents. Typical purposes include account provisioning, localization, incremental chart updates, enforcing business logic (such as preventing duplicate paper trading names), improving the onboarding roadmap, monitoring testing outcomes, and communicating with you about new capabilities.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with trusted processors who help us operate infrastructure (cloud hosting, analytics, email delivery) under confidentiality obligations, with our internal trading services to populate dashboards, or when required by applicable law or legal process. If Tiris undergoes a corporate transaction we may transfer data as part of that event while honoring existing commitments.

## 5. Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use first-party cookies and local storage solely to remember language preferences, maintain authenticated sessions, throttle verification emails, and capture limited opt-in analytics. Browsers provide controls to clear or block these technologies, though doing so may degrade product functionality.

## 6. Data Retention
Account and configuration data are retained while you maintain an active relationship with Tiris and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Paper trading and backtest logs recorded by Tiris are preserved under our internal data retention policies so that charts remain accurate. You may request deletion by contacting us; some residual information may remain in backups for a limited time.

## 7. International Use
Tiris serves a global audience. When you access the portal from outside the jurisdictions where we operate infrastructure, your information may be transferred to, stored in, or processed in those jurisdictions. We take steps to ensure that cross-border transfers remain protected through contractual commitments and technical safeguards.

## 8. Security
Consistent with the architecture documentation, we employ HTTPS, access controls, least-privilege service tokens, encryption for exchange-binding metadata, and monitoring across our internal integrations. No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage you to use unique passwords, enable MFA on connected exchanges, and alert us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, as well as the right to object to direct marketing or data portability. Submit requests through the Contact link in the footer and we will respond in line with applicable regulations.

## 10. Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy as product scope evolves (for example, when authentication, exchange bindings, or paper trading reach new maturity levels). Material revisions will be announced through the portal together with a new effective date. Continued use of the services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
